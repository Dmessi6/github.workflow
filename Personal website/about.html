When I'm not immersed in code, honing my craft, I channel my energy and enthusiasm into the following pursuits.

Drumming

I am deeply passionate about music and aspire to make a significant mark in the industry. Having completed a full season of drum corps, I am enthusiastically planning to continue my journey. My goal isn't just personal achievement; it's also about passing on the invaluable knowledge and experience I've gained to inspire and mentor the upcoming generation of musicians in my future endeavors.

My friends and I had commonly come together to drum, persisting until we reached a point where we couldn't make a mistake. Our excitement and shared desire for improvement were the driving forces behind our endeavors. The joy of those moments when we strived for perfection and growth together created lasting memories that we all hold dear.

Acting

For the past four years, I've dedicated myself to honing my acting skills through consistent and passionate participation in acting classes. This journey has been immensely fulfilling as it allowed me to turn my beloved hobby into a potential source of income. I frequently sought out opportunities, often in collaboration with my agent, to audition for various roles. My enthusiasm for this craft has not waned; I continue to actively search for roles that align with my abilities and passion, eagerly participating in auditions and relishing every chance to bring characters to life.
